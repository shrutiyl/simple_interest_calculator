# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation respectful and inclusive.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful
- Accept feedback
- Be professional

## Enforcement
Violations will be reviewed and addressed.

## Scope
Applies to all contributors.
